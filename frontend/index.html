About us

Passionate about Transforming Spaces

As a trusted, dedicated, and committed family business, we believe in the importance of listening to our clients and understanding their needs, creating customized, high-quality solutions. We are passionate about transforming spaces and improving the quality of life of those who trust us. With years of experience in the renovation industry, our team works diligently and with attention to detail to deliver exceptional results. Our mission is to turn your ideas into reality, creating spaces that reflect your vision and style.

Services

We offer a wide range of services to meet all your
needs.

Installation

Our team of professional installers ensures your renovation is carried out efficiently and with the utmost attention to detail. We ensure that every element is installed correctly and according to your design plans. We oversee every step of the process to ensure the final result not only meets but exceeds your expectations.

Custom Design

At Plum-Mex Showroom, we believe that every renovation project begins with a well-thought-out design. Our team of expert designers works with you to create customized plans that reflect your tastes and needs. From conceptualization to final drawings, we ensure every detail is perfect to transform your ideas into reality.

Supply

We provide all the necessary materials for your renovation project. We collaborate with trusted suppliers to ensure only the highest quality materials are used. From flooring and furniture to the smallest details, we offer a careful selection that meets your expectations and standards
